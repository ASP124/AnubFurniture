ROTARY CLUB EVENT: YEAR 2020

During the Covid Pandemic there was a shortage of blood and with help of Rotary Club of Dombivli Midtown we arranged a Blood Donation camp at Anub Furniture Factory. We could successfully collect 71 bottles of blood during these testing time.

SATARA CHARA DONATION: AUGUST 2019

There was a huge shortage of Fodder for Animals in the flood prone areas and this could have affected their lives and eventually the livelihood of the people. We arranged this much required fodder immediately and dispatched the same to Satara and Sangli area.
